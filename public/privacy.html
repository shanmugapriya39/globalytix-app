Globalytix Translation – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 06 Sep 2025

Contact: support@globalytix.com

Globalytix translates speech and text for classroom use. We do not sell data or use targeted advertising.

Data we process

  • User input (text/audio) you choose to translate. Processed only to return results, not stored server-side.
  • Diagnostics/analytics (e.g., crash logs) may be collected by Apple/third parties to improve stability.
  • No account required. No precise location or background tracking.

Third-party services

We may send your input to speech/translation providers strictly to perform the service.

Retention

We do not retain input beyond what is necessary to deliver a translation. Local history can be cleared in the app.

Children & schools

This app is designed for classroom use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Clear local history in the app.
  • Opt out of analytics via system settings where available.
  • Contact support for questions.

Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is always available at this URL.
